Our Journey & Vision

ISHA CodeCraft Hub is a **Pan-African technology innovation hub** born from a vision to bridge the digital divide and foster localized ICT solutions. With a strong background in embedded systems and technical training, our founder envisioned a dynamic space where **ideas transform into prototypes, students evolve into innovators, and local challenges find tech-driven solutions.**

We are more than just a business; we are a **mission to make African tech global**, starting locally and thinking exponentially. We empower digital transformation by integrating **software development, embedded systems, IoT device engineering, and academic project assistance**.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and industries across Africa by providing **cutting-edge software, embedded systems, and IoT solutions**, bridging academic innovation with real-world implementation and commercial viability.

Our Approach

As a **multi-service tech enterprise** based in Rwanda, we combine **R&D, education support, client services, and hardware prototyping**. This powerful hybrid model allows us to serve both commercial clients and academic scholars with smart, scalable, and sustainable ICT solutions.

Our Key Components

R&D Lab

Conduct cutting-edge research and develop innovative solutions in **Machine Learning, IoT, and Embedded Systems**.

Academic Assistance

Offer **mentorship, prototyping support, coding guidance, and project assistance** to university scholars and researchers.

Client Software Development

Deliver tailored software solutions, including **mobile apps, web platforms, and enterprise tools**, for SMEs, NGOs, and government institutions.

Device Engineering & Production

Design, prototype, and assemble **smart devices like IoT-based systems** for health, agriculture, education, and more.
